Techniques Used



1. Sentiment Analysis

Twitter sentiment analysis enables you to monitor what people are saying about your product or service on social media and can assist you in identifying irate clients or unfavorable remarks before they become more serious.



2. Word Cluster

Word clustering is a technique for organizing a collection of words into groups where the words are semantically related to each other. The majority of research on this issue is in the field of natural language processing, and it can be difficult, particularly for tiny corpora.
